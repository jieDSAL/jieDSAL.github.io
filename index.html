Biography

Jie Xu is an engineer in Huawei Cloud. After he received the Ph.D. degree (supervised by Professor Dan Feng) in computer architecture from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2020, he joins in Huawei Cloud to do research on SSD based software-hardware co-design. He publishes several papers in major journals and international conferences, including TCAD, FGCS, ICCD, DATE, DAC, and MSST.
